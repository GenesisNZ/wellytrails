The Welly Kiwi Rating Scale

How local riders classify our tracks on our classic scale.

EASY AS
Smooth & Cruisy: Flat gravel lines, benched family paths. No vertical risk.
SWEET AS
Relaxed Flow: Mild switchbacks, gentle benched climbs. Safe progression terrain.
CHOICE
Intermediate: Flowing lines, optional tables, fully rollable tech paths with bypasses.
GNARLY
Advanced: Steep chute descents, off-camber loam/clay, fast root lines.
HARDOUT
Expert: High mandatory drops, gap doubles, slick wet clay runs.

Return Climbing Scale

Important for parents with kids or anyone checking their heart rate.

EASY RIDE BACK
Flat or Downhill Return: No climbing needed. Young kids can roll straight back with zero effort.
SWEET AS SPIN
Gentle Grade Climb: Steady benched gradient (2-4%). Easy on young lungs and little legs.
HONEST GRUNT
Steady Incline: Continuous climb with brief steep spots. Shift down and spin it out.
HEAPS AS CLIMBING
Steep Singletrack: Hard work. Young kids will likely need a tow rope or a steady push.
GUTTBUSTER CLIMB
Savage Gradients: Extreme climbs. Prepare to walk or push your rigs up the steepest clay/rock walls.
